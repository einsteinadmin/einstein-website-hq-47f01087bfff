Two kindergarten friends. One old pickup truck.

The whole story — from the sandbox to 10 branches across Texas and Tampa. Still the same two friends. Still 50/50. Still the same promise.

Our story is your standard “two best friends meet in kindergarten, grow up together, and decide to start a moving company.” Oh, you’ve never heard that one before? Well, it’s a classic around here. [live copy, verbatim]

The longer version: Cameron Brown and Paul Morin grew up best friends, roomed together at Texas A&M, and took a few swings after graduating — a nutritional supplement company, a Christmas tree business — while working part-time for a moving company to pay the bills.

That part-time job is where they saw it: the moving industry was clunky, outdated, and deserved its bad reputation. Hidden fees. Coverage that paid by the pound. Crews nobody vetted. Everyone in the industry knew — and nobody was fixing it.

So in 2012, with an old pickup truck, a small business loan, and a lot of determination, they set out to build the moving company they’d actually want to hire. 50/50 from day one, built from scratch — no franchise, no buyout, no shortcuts.

Every part of the Smart Move System traces back to something they hated about the old way: the all-inclusive rate exists because of the hidden-fee game, the $2,000 actual-value coverage because of the 60¢-a-pound insult, Meet Your Movers because nobody should open their door to a stranger.

🌳 Four trees, every move.

Moving uses trucks, fuel, and a small mountain of cardboard — so for every move we complete, we plant four trees with our partner Eden: People + Planet — local crews paid to plant every one. Not a marketing gimmick: trucks burn fuel, and we’re committed to putting back more than we take. A hundred thousand moves later, that’s around 400,000 trees in the ground.
