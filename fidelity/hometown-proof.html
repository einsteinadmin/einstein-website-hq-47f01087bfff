The stuff you're actually wondering

Fair questions. Straight answers.

What happens if my movers are late?

+
You get a discount. Most movers give you a 4–8 hour arrival window and hope for the best. We commit to a 2-hour window — if we miss it, your move is instantly discounted, and the discount grows the longer you wait. We call it our On-Time Guarantee, and we rarely need it.

What does the $2,000 damage coverage actually cover?

+
$2,000 of coverage is included free on every move — based on actual value, not weight, with no deductible. (The industry default is 60¢/lb: a $30 check for a broken $1,000 TV.) A few honest exceptions:
  • Particle board furniture: covered at $0.60/lb only — it's inherently fragile.
  • Owner-packed boxes: if you packed it, we can't guarantee what's inside.
  • Appliances: disconnect/reconnect requires a signed liability waiver.
  • PODs or rental trucks: coverage applies when we're driving our trucks.

Can I cancel my move?

+
Yes — free cancellation, no deposit required to book. Plans change, we get it. Just give us as much notice as you can.

Who's showing up at my door?

+
No strangers showing up unannounced. A few days before your move, we send your crew's names, photos, and bios. Every Einstein mover is a background-checked, W-2 employee — no subcontractors, ever.

How long does a North Austin move take?

+
Moves in North Austin take 2.5 hours minimum, about 7 hours on average. Larger moves, longer timelines — time depends on size, distance, and number of stops. We'll give you a realistic estimate upfront so there are no guessing games.
