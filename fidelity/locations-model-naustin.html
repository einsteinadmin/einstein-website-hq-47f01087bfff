Fair questions. Straight answers.

What if my movers are late?
If your movers are late, you get a discount! Most movers give you a 4–8 hour arrival window and hope for the best. We commit to a 2-hour window. If we miss it, you get a discount that grows the longer you wait. We call it our On-Time Guarantee, and we rarely need it.
What if something breaks?
$2,000 damage coverage. Included. Free. The industry default is 60 cents per pound — that’s like giving $30 for breaking a $1,000 TV. We cover your belongings the way they should be covered, on every single move, at no extra cost. Exactly what’s covered (and the honest edges) is all on our Coverage page →
How long does moving in North Austin take?
Moves in North Austin take either 2.5 hours minimum or 7 hours on average. Larger moves, longer timelines. Move time depends on size, distance, and the number of stops. We’ll give you a realistic estimate upfront so there’s no guessing games!
Who’s showing up at my door?
No strangers showing up unannounced. A few days before your move, we send your crew’s names, photos, and bios. All background-checked, all pros.
Can I cancel my move?
Yes, free cancellation. No deposit required to book. Plans change, we get it. Just give us as much notice as you can.
How can I save money when hiring movers?
The best way to save money on a move is to reduce what moving crews have to handle. The more prep you do before move day, the faster the move, and the lower the bill.
  • Disassemble complex furniture like bunk beds or workout equipment ahead of time.
  • Box up as much as possible before we arrive.
  • Move small misc items (lamps, wall art, garage items) yourself in your own car.
  • Declutter first — don’t pay to move things you don’t need!
  • Take advantage of free boxes.
What if my new place has stairs, a freight elevator, or tricky access?
No worries, our crews are trained for stairs and places with tricky access. Just mention any access quirks (narrow hallways, walk-up floors, parking restrictions) when you book so we can plan ahead and avoid any surprises on move day.
Do you move specialty items like pianos, safes, or large furniture?
Due to liability concerns, we’re unable to move items that weigh more than 400 lbs (with certain exceptions), and we don’t move grand or baby grand pianos. Upright pianos are fine as long as they’re not going up or down stairwells. Pool tables must be disassembled with the slates removed. No vehicle transport (cars, golf carts, dirt bikes, riding mowers), and nothing perishable, prohibited, or hazardous. Unsure about your item? Contact us and we’ll help.
Are packing materials included?
We can provide boxes, wrapping paper, bubble wrap, and tape — we do charge for these, but since we buy in bulk, our pricing often beats anything you’ll find elsewhere. If we’re doing the packing, we bring the supplies on move day and you just pay for what we used. We don’t deliver boxes, but you’re welcome to come by the branch and buy them from our wonderful staff. Your own supplies? Happy to use them, as long as they’re new or gently used (emphasis on gently).
How do I know you’re legit?
Fair question — this industry earned the skepticism. Every Texas mover is required to register with the Texas Department of Motor Vehicles and carry liability for damaged or lost goods. Don’t take our word for it: look us up in the TxDMV database with our USDOT number, 2193963. (Florida folks: our FL USDOT is 3882313.)
