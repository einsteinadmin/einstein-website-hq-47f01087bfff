The fine print, in plain English

What forms of payment do you accept?

+

All major credit cards, with no card fees. In Texas we’re cards only; in Florida we take cards and cash. Companies paying by check for an office move can contact us ahead of time to confirm.

How far in advance should I book?

+

The earlier the better — especially summer and end-of-month dates. For long-distance moves within Texas we recommend 2–4 weeks out. And since there’s no deposit and cancellation is free, booking early costs you nothing.

What happens if something breaks?

+

$2,000 damage coverage. Included, free, on every move. The industry default is 60 cents per pound — that’s a $30 check for a broken $1,000 TV. We cover your belongings based on what they’re actually worth, with no deductible.

How do I get a quote?

+

Online, in about 47 seconds, with zero phone calls. You’ll see your full hourly rate before anything is scheduled — and the rate you see is the rate you pay.
